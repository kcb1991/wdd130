History

Founded in 1985, Rapid Runners began as a passion project for three thrill-seekers—Mike, Sarah, and Jorge—who wanted to share their love of Washington's wild rivers with adventurous souls. Their first guided whitewater trip launched on the Skykomish River, quickly earning them a reputation for adrenaline-pumping rapids and top-notch safety. By the early 2000s, the company had expanded its operations to the Wenatchee and White Salmon Rivers, offering trips for all experience levels—from first-time paddlers to hardcore rafters seeking Class V thrills. They became pioneers in river conservation, advocating for sustainable rafting practices to keep Washington’s waterways pristine. Today, Rapid Runners is known for its expert guides, cutting-edge rafting gear, and commitment to creating unforgettable river experiences. Whether it’s navigating churning rapids or floating through serene canyons, they continue to lead the way for adventurers across the Pacific Northwest.
