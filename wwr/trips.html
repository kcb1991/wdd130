Washington's Wildest Whitewater Adventures

Wenatchee River Whitewater Adventure

Blast through Class III+ rapids with sun-soaked waves and breathtaking Cascades views. This full-day trip is perfect for beginners craving excitement—plus riverside lunch and lots of splash zones!

White Salmon Canyon Run

A pulse-pounding ride through a narrow canyon with Class IV–V rapids and a 10-foot optional waterfall drop! For adventurers who want intensity and unforgettable scenery.

Spokane River Scenic & Splash Tour

Perfect for families or first-time paddlers! Spot wildlife, enjoy calm stretches with a few fun rapids, and drift through the lush beauty of Riverside State Park as the sun sets around you.
